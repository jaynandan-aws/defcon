Salesforce Service Cloud Implementation

TESTMiNDS helps you optimize your customer service, gain social capital by leveraging social media

Our implementation service will empower your customer by moving away from the legacy customer service applicaiton to a more productive cloud based Salesforce.com Service cloud applicaiton and enable a relationship based solution strategy to drive growth.

The new system would have customer management, case management, knowledge management and web portal capabilities.

Our Salesforce Service Cloud implementation includes:

  • A business process review focused around the service department processes to identify the needs of the system and the best process.
  • Case Management
  • Configure Email to Case so you can have emails sent to something like support@yourcompany.com will create a case automatically
  • Web to Case webform configuration to embed on your website.
  • Creating Pricebooks and adding prices to your products
  • Escalation Rules.
  • Validation Rules
  • Knowledge Base or Solutions to be setup.
  • Configure Service Cloud Console with the layouts/records needed for your process
  • Service Contracts and Entitlements
  • Development of a Standard Operating Procedure document that outlines how to perform your process in Salesforce.
  • Customize fields to meet your needs. Assuming basic customizations of standard fields like Lead Status, Opportunity Stages, Opportunity Types, and a few custom fields needed unique to your company
  • Training
